Use the book to learn the Basics of 3D Printing and Design

3D Printing Step by Step

As the pace of technological innovation begins to quicken, in times like these, 3D Design and Printing is a great skill to have in your armoury. This book will serve as a getting started guide to 3D printing terminology and take you step by step to designing a 3D object using a simple web-based program called Tinkercad and finally printing the same 3D object. In addition, common mistakes with 3D printing and the solutions chapter make the whole learning process more effortless. And also serves as a reference if issues occur with your 3D prints.
