3D Printing

3D printing, or additive manufacturing, is the construction of a three-dimensional object from a Computer-Aided Design(CAD) model or a digital 3D model. The term "3D printing" can refer to a variety of processes in which material is deposited, joined, or solidified under computer control to create a three-dimensional object, with the material being added together (such as plastics, liquids, or powder grains being fused together), typically layer by layer.
