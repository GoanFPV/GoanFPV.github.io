3D Printing

3D printing, or additive manufacturing, is the construction of a three-dimensional object from a CAD (computer-aided design) model or a digital 3D model. The term "3D printing" can refer to a variety of processes in which material is deposited, joined or solidified under computer control to create a three-dimensional object, with material being added together (such as plastics, liquids or powder grains being fused together), typically layer by layer.

LED Christmas tree

This is a great way to modify and add that extra wow! factor to your christmas holiday wreath that hangs outside your front door. Here we are using an Arduino MKR1000, NeoPixels, and a buzzer. And we also get notified via SMS/email when someone is at the door. Also add a piezo buzzer to play your favorite holiday tune.
