Drone Photography, 3D Printing services, Electronics Trainings/Workshops and more ... by Goan FPV

We are a family shop based out of South Goa, India, with a focus in providing services related to Drones/Custom Quadcopter Photography and Videography, 3D printing services using Fused Deposition Modeling(FDM) printers using PLA and ABS materials. In addition, we provide 3D printing and basic electronics Training and Workshops for-

  • 3D Printing and Design
  • Learn basic electronics using Arduino
  • Learn programming using the Raspberry Pi
  • IoT jumpstart course

Since many folks have already asked us why the website name contains FPV, which stand for First Person View. Basically, this website was first developed to work on custom FPV Quadcopter/Drones for aerial Photography and Videography. Later on, as folks were instrested in learning new technologies they demanded for Training/Workshops related to 3D Printing and Electronics

Please note, due to COVID-19 the class size has been reduced 10 students because of Government restrictions, and we are currently not able to travel to neighbouring States.
