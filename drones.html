Aerial Photography and Videography by Goan FPV

Drones/ Quadcopter

Drone Photography and videography is a widespread technique chosen for event coverages like weddings/festivals, Tourism promotion, real estate advertisement, etc.

We also build custom First Person View(FPV) Quadcopters to mount a GoPro Hero7 black to take advantage of 4K video and HyperSmooth, which is equivalent to Gimbal-like stabilization–without the gimbal. And also consult on buliding FPV Quadcopter for high-speed racing to cutting-edge freestyle.

Check out some of our projects/custom builds to get inspired.
